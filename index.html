OX GAME

This game is "OX GAME".This game is extremely simple. It involves two players competing against each other to be the first to form a line vertically, horizontally, or diagonally. The player who achieves this first is the winner.

Play it!

OSERO

This game is "OSERO".This is a game where players place their own colored stones on a board, strategically sandwiching their opponent's colored stones. By doing so, they can flip the sandwiched stones to their own color. This process continues alternately, and in the end, the player with the most stones of their own color wins.

Play it!
